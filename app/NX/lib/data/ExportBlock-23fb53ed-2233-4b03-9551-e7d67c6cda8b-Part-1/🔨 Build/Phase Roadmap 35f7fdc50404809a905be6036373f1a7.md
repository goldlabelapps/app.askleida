**Potential Future Phases — order not yet decided**

**Ask Leida** An in-app AI chatbot giving practitioners instant answers to mid-appointment skincare questions. Powered by a curated clinical knowledge base using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) architecture — this grounds Leida's answers in verified sources rather than general internet data, significantly reducing the risk of AI hallucination. The knowledge base will ideally draw from trusted clinical references including Dermnet, NHS guidance and peer-reviewed ingredient science.

**Leida Studio** A premium creative tier giving practitioners personalisation options for their PDF recommendations — brand colours, fonts, logo placement — alongside AI-styled product photography, educational skin diagram generation, and social media content creation tools. Includes personalised tone of voice options so the AI writes in a style that reflects each practitioner's individual communication style.

**Client Facing Portal and Skin Tracker** A dedicated space where practitioners can share recommendations directly with clients, track skin progress over time and communicate aftercare instructions — replacing the current WhatsApp workflow. Includes a client-facing view of recommendation history and a skin tracker allowing practitioners to log changes, photograph progress and document treatment outcomes over time. This phase strengthens the proprietary dataset as Leida will not only collect the recommendations sent by the practitioner but also the results!

**Automated Aftercare Sequences** Triggered communications sent to clients automatically (in app) after a treatment — based on treatment type, products recommended and time elapsed. Reduces the practitioner's admin burden while ensuring clients receive timely, relevant aftercare guidance. Contributes to the compliance documentation trail.

**Booking System Integration** Integration with existing booking platforms — Fresha as the primary target — giving Leida read access to appointment data. Enables smarter recommendation timing, automated aftercare triggers and a more complete picture of each client's treatment history within Leida.

**A note on database architecture and the data asset**

Every recommendation generated in Leida contributes to a proprietary anonymised dataset of professionally verified skincare recommendation decisions — which products were recommended, for which skin concerns, by qualified practitioners, at the point of consultation. This dataset has significant long term commercial value and cannot be replicated quickly by competitors.

This is not a visible feature. It accumulates as a byproduct of normal practitioner use. However it has direct implications for how the database schema is designed from day one. Every field Chris builds should be considered through the lens of: which of the three databases does this belong to, and does it contribute to the proprietary dataset?

The three databases are documented separately in the Database Architecture page.
