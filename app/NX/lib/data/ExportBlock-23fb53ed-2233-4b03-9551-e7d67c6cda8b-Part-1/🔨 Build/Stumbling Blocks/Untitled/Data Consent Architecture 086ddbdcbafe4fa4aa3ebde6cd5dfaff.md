# Data Consent Architecture

Existential Risk: Consent not structured at point of collection to permit secondary use of anonymised data. Retrofitting consent after users are live destroys data asset value in due diligence.
Mitigation Required: Legal review of sign-up terms and consent language before first real user. Consent must explicitly cover anonymised secondary use. Chris to build consent capture into onboarding flow as a structural element, not a checkbox afterthought.
