# International Expansion Compliance

Existential Risk: Crossing borders triggers complex data localisation requirements, different consent regimes and potentially HIPAA obligations in the US. Moving too fast internationally before UK architecture is hardened creates compounding compliance debt.
Mitigation Required: UK beachhead first — establish GDPR compliance and prove product-market fit before any international expansion. Australia and Canada are natural second markets (shared language, similar solo-operator dynamics). US is the ultimate prize but requires dedicated legal preparation. Do not expand until UK architecture passes external data protection audit.
