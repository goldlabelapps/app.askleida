# Free Trial and Subscribe via Stripe

Category: Payments
Code: PAY 01
Done When:   • 14 day free trial available on sign up — no credit card required
• Trial period clearly communicated during onboarding — day count visible in the app
• On day 15 Stripe automatically charges the chosen tier — practitioner is prompted to select tier and add card before trial ends
• Reminder notification sent at day 10 — 4 days remaining on your trial
• Reminder notification sent at day 13 — your trial ends tomorrow
• If practitioner adds card and selects tier before day 15 — subscription activates immediately
• If practitioner does not add card by day 15 — access pauses, not deleted. Data retained. Clear prompt to reactivate.
• Founding Member (£29), Standard (£39) and Unlimited (£59) tiers available at conversion (TBC)
• Failed payment shows clear error with option to retry
• Practitioner receives confirmation email on subscription activation
Phase: Phase 1
Status: Not started
Story: As a practitioner, I want to try Leida free for 14 days without entering my card details, so that I can experience the full product before committing to a subscription.
