# Referral Dashboard

Category: Referrals
Code: REF 01
Done When:   • Practitioner has a unique referral link available in their account
• Referral link can be copied and shared via WhatsApp, email or any channel
• Dashboard shows number of referrals sent, number who signed up for trial, number who converted to paid
• Practitioner receives one free month of their current tier for every referral who converts to a paid subscription
• Free month applied automatically to next billing cycle — no manual action required (is this possible?)
• Practitioner notified when a referral converts — "someone you referred just joined Leida"
• Free months stack — referring three people gives three free months
• Referral attribution tracked for 30 days — if someone signs up within 30 days of clicking the link it counts
Phase: Phase 1
Status: Not started
Story: As a practitioner, I want to share a unique referral link with colleagues and track how many have signed up through my recommendation, so that I am rewarded for helping Leida grow.
