> [CleverText text="For Ask Leida, I’d lean into a palette that feels:"]

* intelligent but calm
* clinical-adjacent without looking medical
* feminine without becoming “beauty brand pink”
* trustworthy in both light and dark mode
* traditional enough to age well over 5–10 years

A strong direction is a muted sage / soft teal primary with warm neutrals and restrained accent colours.

## Suggested Typography Pairing

* Headings: `Cormorant Garamond` or `Fraunces`
* UI/Text: `Inter`

That combination gives:

* premium editorial feel
* modern app usability
* beauty/wellness alignment
* strong AI-product credibility

---

## Overall Feel

Visually this would sit somewhere between:

* premium skincare software
* calm AI assistant
* boutique wellness brand
* modern clinical dashboard

—not startup-neon, cyberpunk, or overly “tech bro”.

## Example Usage Split

* 70% warm neutrals
* 20% sage/teal primary
* 10% accents

That restraint is what keeps it looking expensive.
