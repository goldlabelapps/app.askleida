#### High-Trust Solo Technical Operator

A very real emerging category of role is 

Someone who can:
- understand business problems
- architect systems
- ship quickly
- use AI leverage effectively
- coordinate specialists only when needed
- deliver outcomes without organisational drag

That model can absolutely work now because AI massively increases individual throughput.

The key change is this:

Previously:
- bigger project → bigger team

Now:
- bigger project → better operator


#### Fractional Technical Partner

Probably the strongest fit for experienced developers right now.

You become:
- interim CTO
- product lead
- technical strategy + delivery
- architecture + execution

Target:
- startups
- SMEs
- funded non-technical founders
- traditional businesses modernising internally

They often do *not* want an agency.  

They want:
- one accountable person
- low politics
- speed
- sane pricing

This is especially powerful if you can:
- prototype in days
- automate aggressively
- communicate commercially

#### AI-Enabled Micro Agency

Not a traditional agency with payroll and PM layers.

More like:
- you
- maybe 1–3 trusted specialists
- contractors on-demand
- AI handling huge amounts of production

You stay lean intentionally.

The advantage:
- agencies still charge based on old labour assumptions
- you can undercut while maintaining margin

This space is still very under-defined.

- categorisation
- reporting
- workflow automation

are suddenly cheap to implement.

#### Specialist Integration / Operator Role

Massive demand is forming around:
- connecting systems

- AI workflow integration
- replacing spreadsheets and manual admin

They need:
- operational simplification
- automation

This is often much higher ROI than app-building.
