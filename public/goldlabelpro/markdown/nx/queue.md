> [CleverText text="Why Do We Need a Queue?"] 

Sometimes there are too many tasks for the app to handle all at once. The queue helps by organising these tasks in the order they arrive, so each one gets done, one after the other. This makes sure nothing is forgotten and everything is handled fairly.

- **Adding Tasks:** When there is something new to do, it gets added to the end of the queue.
- **Processing Tasks:** The app takes the first task in the queue and works on it. When it’s done, it moves to the next one.
- **Staying Organized:** The queue keeps everything in order, so tasks are not missed or done out of turn.

> Why?

- **Fairness:** Every task gets its turn, just like people in a line.
- **Efficiency:** The app doesn’t get overwhelmed by trying to do everything at once.
- **Reliability:** Tasks are not lost or forgotten, so the system works smoothly.
