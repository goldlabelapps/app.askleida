> The phrase **PORNVIRUSMP3** originates from the early work of British designer and artist Paul Khera in the late 1990s.  

According to documentation in The New International, Khera encountered the slogan while collaborating with artists in Russia, Ukraine, and the Baltics. 

The Concept: Khera noted that the phrase was viewed as a "list of things that were defining the internet at the time," highlighting how the web's cultural impact was being shaped by these three elements regardless of its original intended functions. 

He saw this as a parallel to the bold, direct slogans found in early 1990s Russian gig posters, comparing it to a gritty, internet-era version of corporate slogans like Nike's "Just Do It"
